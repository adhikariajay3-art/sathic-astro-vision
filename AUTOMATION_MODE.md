# SATHIC ASTRO VISION - Automation Mode

Automation mode means the business has repeatable daily operating rules.

## Automation Stack

1. Store Page
   - File: digital-product-store.html
   - Purpose: customer selects product and sends order to WhatsApp.

2. Payment Confirmation
   - File: payment-confirmation.html
   - Purpose: customer copies payment details after UPI payment.

3. Order Dashboard
   - File: order-dashboard.html
   - Purpose: track paid, pending and delivered orders.

4. Lead CRM
   - File: lead-tracking-crm.html
   - Purpose: track new leads, follow-ups and conversions.

5. Revenue Dashboard
   - File: revenue-dashboard.html
   - Purpose: track yearly target and income sources.

6. WhatsApp Funnel
   - File: whatsapp-funnel-manager.html
   - Purpose: copy daily follow-up messages.

## Daily Automation Routine

Morning:
- Create 3 content posts.
- Share store link on WhatsApp status and groups.
- Add new leads in Lead CRM.

Afternoon:
- Follow up pending leads.
- Check payment screenshots.
- Add verified payments to Order Dashboard.

Evening:
- Deliver paid PDF or report.
- Update delivery status.
- Update Revenue Dashboard.
- Send membership upsell message.
